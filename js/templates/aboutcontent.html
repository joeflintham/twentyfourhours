24 Hours At Jono's

About 24 Hours at Jono's

24 hours at Jono's is an audio visual documentary exploring the micro community that exists at Jono's Snooker Club in Camberwell, London. Spoken word narration, interviews, video, storytelling and live sound recordings are used to recreate the atmosphere of Jono's Snooker Club. Our aim was to highlight this micro community to a wider audience, inviting outsiders to explore a space they may not usually be a part of.

Creators
