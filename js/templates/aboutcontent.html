24 Hours At Jono's

About 24 Hours at Jono's

24 hours at Jono's is an audio visual documentary exploring the micro community that exists at Jono's Snooker Club in Camberwell, London. Spoken word narration, interviews, video, storytelling and live sound recordings are used to recreate the atmosphere of Jono's Snooker Club. Our aim was to highlight this micro community to a wider audience, inviting outsiders to explore a space they may not usually be a part of.

Roundhouse

This project was created as part of the Visualised Audio Fund, a Roundhouse and Ideas Tap project supporting young creatives to develop innovative work at the junction of radio, moving image, digital media and storytelling. The fund is part of the Roundhouse's programme of creative opportunities for 11-25 year-olds. Each year the Roundhouse helps 3,000 11-25 year-olds from all backgrounds to realise their creative potential through music, media and performing arts. Much of this work takes place in the Paul Hamlyn Roundhouse Studios - a dedicated creative centre for music and media production, broadcasting, performances and rehearsal, where young people work with industry professionals to learn and develop their skills.
 
Find out more at www.roundhouse.org.uk.

IdeasTap

IdeasTap is a not for profit creative network that funds, connects and nurtures new talent. It was launched in December 2008 as the arts platform of The Peter De Haan Charitable Trust and has since given over £850,000 in direct cash funding and accompanying expert mentoring to young creative people. Its membership currently stands at more than 90,000 young creative practitioners and its partners include Old Vic New Voices, the National Youth Theatre of Great Britain, Magnum Photos, The British Film Institute, HighTide, The National Student Drama Festival, MAC Birmingham, Roundhouse, Bush Theatre, Brighton Fringe and The National Skills Academy for Creative and Cultural. The website provides a platform for young creative people to connect, showcase their work and skills, find jobs, and promote events and exhibitions, as well as access unique industry-led opportunities.
 
For more information about IdeasTap go to www.ideastap.com.
